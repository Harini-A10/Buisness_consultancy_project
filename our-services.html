Our Services

Specification / Design

STRABUS consultants conduct onsite spec-writing and HLD activities, starting with business document review and user interviews. They prepare Preliminary and Detailed Analysis Documents with context diagrams, DFDs, ERDs, logical and physical models, test plans, and cases. Prototyping follows either the design phase or completion of the logical model, ensuring thorough system documentation and validation.

Design / Prototyping (partly onsite)

As part of our services, STRABUS can provide design & prototyping services. Our consultants retain skills in using RAD tools for prototyping. Based on an application design, they can quickly create prototypes that go with necessary documentation. Our methodology ensures that prototyping / application development is carried out using templates. These templates are developed in-house. Our consultants retain sound skills in both SSAD and OOAD techniques.

Code-writing / Programming

STRABUS handles prototyping, development, coding, testing, and documentation with strict quality standards. Code maintainability is ensured through agreed structures and walkthroughs. Rigorous 3rd party testing with SQATest, WinRunner, and PVCS version control ensures reliability, consistency, and excellence in every project.


Turnkey Projects

STRABUS excels in client/server and net-centric architectures, leveraging mainstream tools, databases, and middleware. With proven expertise in financial, inventory, distribution, and healthcare systems, consultants transform user requirements into reliable, on-time, and cost-effective solutions. Skilled in Oracle, Microsoft, and Sun technologies, STRABUS consistently delivers high-quality, functional systems that exceed client expectations.

  • Scope of QMS

    The ISO 9001 aids in the standardization of the way in which an organization intends to meet the requirements of its customers, follow manufacturing practices and maintain records. It requires the organization to set down its objectives and commitments to, or its policy on, quality. Every activity of the company must be identified and standardized, prescribing in detail the route and rules that each procedure has to follow and the check posts that have to be set up en route. These templates for each of the processes in the development of the product are set down in the companyâ€™s Quality Management Systems Manual. The emphasis here is on the prevention of nonconformity rather than on the taking of corrective action to amend it.

  • Software Development Life Cycle

    The software engineering process at STRABUS consists of three generic phases: definition, development and maintenance. First, the business analyst contacts the customer and analyses his explicit and potential requirements. Then these requirement specifications are documented and project management, software development and software configuration management plans made. Based on these, the design document is drawn up. The software programmer then translates these specifications into computer language, i.e. coding and testing. Finally, there are corrections, adaptations, enhancement and re-engineering done by the programmer.
